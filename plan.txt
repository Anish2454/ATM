Classes: BankAccount, Main(ATM)

BankAccount should have a balance, pin, and a userName. Should be able to transfer money to
another account. Should also be able to accept a transfer from another account. Should also be
able to withdraw and deposit money. Shouldn't allow account to go into debt.

The Atm should be able to complete the transfers between accounts and allow the user to
withdraw and deposit money to their account. It should only allow these methods to be called
once the correct pin has been entered. Should save list of all accounts.
